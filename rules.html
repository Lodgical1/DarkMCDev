DarkMC Dev Rules

These rules of DarkMC will be firmly put in place when upon joining the DarkMC Dev world

Please fall under the Minecraft EULA (End User Licence Agreement)

You are not allowed to be racist in any way.

You are not allowed to say anything homaphobic or sexual on DarkMC

You are not allowed to harass and bully others. This server is supposed to be a welcoming place for people of all ages.

You are not allowed to post anything about someone or ruin someones reputation on DarkMC

Please fall under the DarkMC Dev standard rules

You will be permanetly suspended from the Dev team of DarkMC

(SUPER RARE CHANCE)
